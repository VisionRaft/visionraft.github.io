Semi-immersive VR

Offers users a mix of real and virtual interactive elements usually for educational purposes.

Augmented Reality (AR)

Doesnâ€™t fully immerse the user, but it differs from semi-immersive VR in that it overlays virtual and real elements. It finds its applications in education, retail, and entertainment.

Collaborative VR

Can bring people closer together than before. This form of VR projects the same virtual environment to several users at once, no matter how far apart they may be. As a result, they can share ideas and work together on projects.

Mixed Reality

Is similar to AR in that it combines virtual and real elements. Unlike AR, however, the virtual components in MR interact with the real world, leading to an authentic affected VR experience.

PROJECT VISION

Early Eduation, Evolved

Problem

The deprivation of the children and adolescent youth from social interactions and nourishing activities especially done with their peers during the quarantine may cause issues for the development of their social, mental, emotional, and cognitive skills as well as their physical abilities.

Solution

PROJECT VISION is a VR-powered platform consisting of different hubs to help children and adolescent youth nurture their social, mental, emotional, and cognitive skills as well as their physical abilities while being isolated during and even after the current Pandemic.
